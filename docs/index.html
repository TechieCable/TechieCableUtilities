TechieCableUtilities

A compilation of TechieCable's favorite tricks written with AutoHotkey. The entire program is written in AutoHotkey, including the installer and launcher.
Download!

AlwaysOnTop

Pin a program "on top" of other programs!
View a window above another by pressing the hotkey to pin it.

TouchPadToggle

For computers with a touchpad, but no easy way to toggle!
Press the hotkey to turn the touchpad on/off.

Special Characters

Easily type character not available on the standard keyboard.
Spanish accented characters (áéíóú), Spanish punctionuation (¿¡!?),
math symbols (×÷≥≤), and number superscripts (x¹²³⁴) are easy to type.

Need a special character included?
TechieCableUtilities supports custom special characters as well!
